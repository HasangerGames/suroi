Happy New Year! As night falls on the last day of 2025, celebrate the holiday with this year's limited time **New Year's Eve event**, where airdrops spawn every 30 seconds.
***
- **New Year's Eve event!** Darkened, smaller version of the winter map with airdrops spawning every 30 seconds. Rare loot is more common.
- Rebalanced Icicle Bow:
  - Reload time halved (3.2s -> 1.6s)
  - Removed slowdown effect
  - Recoil increased (0.95x multiplier -> 0.9x)
  - Recoil time increased (150ms -> 200ms)
