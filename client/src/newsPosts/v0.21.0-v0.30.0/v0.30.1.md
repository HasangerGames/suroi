**Winter** mode is back! Explore the new **Igloo** and impale your adversaries with the new **Icicle Bow**. Slide across rivers and ocean with the new **ice physics** mechanic. Enjoy and happy holidays!
***
# Suroi v0.30.1
- **Winter mode update!** Reskins for buildings and obstacles have been updated.
- **New building!** Added the **Igloo**, which spawns commonly throughout the map.
- **New gun!** Added the **Icicle Bow**, a powerful crossbow with one shot and infinite ammo.
- **Ice physics!** All water on the island in winter mode is now ice.
- **Gun stats!** Added options to display stats in the pickup message for a gun.
- **Cooler graphics settings!** Added a new tab to the settings for configuring cooler graphics.
- Fixed pan stoves not spawning in lighthouse and bear bunker.
